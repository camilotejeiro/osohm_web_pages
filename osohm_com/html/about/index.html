Osohm brings sound economics to open technology

Our mission is to build a solid economic model to establish open source1 as the de-facto standard for new technologies. We provide an investment platform to facilitate the funding of open projects directly by informed consumers.

Open technology guarantees a better return, It provides greater value to the individual plus a substantial economic return to the community.

Our social goal is our reason for being. Our dearest hope is to open technical knowledge to educate consumers and reduce the knowledge gap. This will in turn create more opportunities for all and reduce income inequality.

1 We are referring to open source meaning open intellectual property movements in general, not in reference to any particular organization.
